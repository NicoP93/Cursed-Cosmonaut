Curse of the Phantom Cosmonaut

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. By clicking each of the phantoms below you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose if your score goes above this number.



The value of each phantom is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time the game starts, the values of the phantoms will be changed
